Chocolate Souffle

Home

Description

A delicious chocolate souffle that is sure to impress your guests.

Ingredients

  • Dark chocolate
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Melt the dark chocolate and butter together in a heatproof bowl over simmering water.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks and sugar until pale and thick.
  4. Fold the melted chocolate mixture into the egg yolk mixture.
  5. In another bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  6. Gently fold the egg whites into the chocolate mixture.
  7. Sift in the flour and fold until just combined.
  8. Pour the batter into greased ramekins and bake for 12-15 minutes.
  9. Serve immediately with a dusting of powdered sugar or a scoop of ice cream.
