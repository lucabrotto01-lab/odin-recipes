Crepes

Description

Let's cook some delicious crepes! They are so easy to make and always a hit!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk the milk, eggs, and melted butter until well combined.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. The batter will be thin.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled non-stick skillet or crepe pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter into the pan and swirl to coat the bottom evenly.
